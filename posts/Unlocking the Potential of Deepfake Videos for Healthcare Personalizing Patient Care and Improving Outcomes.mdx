Deepfake videos can  create personalized and  relatable patient  education materials, fostering a  deeper understanding of  complex medical conditions and treatment options. Deepfake technology offers medical students and healthcare professionals an innovative  approach to learning  and skill development. This immersive learning experience enhances comprehension, retention, and the development  of critical thinking abilities ultimately improving the quality of patient  care.

### Clinical Decision-Making: Enhancing Diagnostic Accuracy  and Treatment  Planning

Deepfake videos can  revolutionize clinical  decision-making by providing healthcare professionals with  patient-specific virtual consultations.
