## **What is Deepfake Technology?**

Deepfakes are created using a  variety of techniques but  the  most common is generative adversarial networks  (GANs). GANs  are two neural networks that compete with each other to create realistic-looking images or videos. The first network, the generator, creates a fake image or video, while the second network,  the discriminator, tries to distinguish the fake  from the real.

As the two networks  compete the  generator learns to create increasingly realistic fakes, while the discriminator learns to identify more subtle differences  between  real and fake content. This  process  continues until the generator is able to create fakes that are indistinguishable from  the real thing.

* **Reputation  damage:** Deepfakes can  be used to damage  the reputation of individuals or organizations.
